## Why HelmLiteracy?

A helm is the wheel that steers a ship. We chose the name because financial and technological literacy is exactly that — the ability to steer your own course instead of being pushed around by currents you don't understand.

Most people weren't taught how money really works. And now, artificial intelligence is reshaping every industry faster than most of us can keep up with. HelmLiteracy exists to close both gaps.

## What We Write About

**Personal Finance**
- Building a budget that actually sticks
- Getting out of debt strategically
- Understanding your credit score and how to improve it
- Investing for beginners — index funds, retirement accounts, and more

**AI Tools & Literacy**
- How to use AI tools like ChatGPT, Claude, and others productively
- Understanding what AI can and can't do
- Spotting AI-generated misinformation
- Practical AI workflows for work, studying, and everyday life

**Investing Basics**
- Stocks, ETFs, and index funds explained simply
- Roth IRA vs. traditional IRA — which is right for you?
- Dollar-cost averaging and why it works
- How to read a company's financials without an MBA

## Our Philosophy

- **No jargon without explanation.** Every technical term gets defined the first time we use it.
- **Honest about what we don't know.** We're not licensed financial advisors. We share research, frameworks, and perspectives — not personalized advice.
- **Practical over theoretical.** Every post ends with something you can actually do.
