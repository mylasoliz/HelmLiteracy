## Who I Am

Hi! My name is Myla Soliz. I am a rising senior at Claremont McKenna College studying Economics and Psychology. As a first-generation college student, I quickly realized my comprehension gap when it came to financial literacy. From discovering what a Roth IRA is to understanding the distinctions between an Index fund and an individual stock, I spent way too much time compiling resources to educate myself on everything from the basics to the topics of financial literacy they don't cover in school. This process inspired me to simplify the learning experience for others who are either starting from ground zero or seeking to deepen their current understanding.

Similar to my experience with becoming financially literate, I recognized the importance of AI literacy in our rapidly evolving digital era in the age of Artificial Intelligence. I am in the process of teaching myself the fundamentals of AI and am even experimenting with vibe coding (this blog)! In my opinion, AI literacy is the language of the modern age, and I believe that everyone should have the opportunity to become fluent.

My mission with HelmLiteracy is to offer resources and support in educating anyone, from my thirteen-year-old cousin to my sixty-two-year-old nani, about the fundamentals, significant developments, and practical applications of finance and AI. I'm everlearning and would love to connect with like-minded individuals on just about everything.

## Our Philosophy

- **No jargon without explanation.** Every technical term gets defined the first time we use it.
- **Honest about what we don't know.** We're not licensed financial advisors. We share research, frameworks, and perspectives — not personalized advice.
- **Practical over theoretical.** Every post ends with something you can actually do.
